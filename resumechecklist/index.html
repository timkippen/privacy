Privacy Policy

ResumeChecklist App
Effective February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the ResumeChecklist app (the "App"), developed in partnership with Cal Poly Pomona College of Engineering, collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to function with minimal data collection. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, or location data. All checklist data you enter is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

2. Data Storage

All data created within the App is stored exclusively on your device. We do not use cloud storage, external servers, or any remote databases. If you delete the App, all associated data will be permanently removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

4. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics tools, tracking technologies, cookies, or similar mechanisms to monitor your usage or behavior.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal information from any user, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the App or on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
